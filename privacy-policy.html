Privacy Policy for DosePulse

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

Medical Disclaimer: DosePulse is not a medical device. This app is intended for informational and personal tracking purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of a physician or other qualified health provider with any questions regarding a medical condition or supplement regimen.

1. Introduction

DosePulse ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data when you use our mobile application. Because we believe your health data is your own, we have designed DosePulse to minimize data collection.

2. Health Data (Supplement Logs)

DosePulse does not collect your supplement logs. All information you input regarding supplement names, dosages, and schedules is stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers or shared with any third party.

3. Data We Collect (Functionality & Subscriptions)

To provide "Pro" features and manage subscriptions, we collect the following limited information via our service provider, RevenueCat:

  • Purchase History: Information about your subscription status and transaction dates. We do NOT have access to your credit card or bank details; these are handled exclusively by Google Play.
  • Identifiers: An anonymous Device ID used to verify your subscription and allow for "Restore Purchases" functionality across device reinstalls.

4. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. RevenueCat receives anonymous identifiers and purchase tokens to validate your subscription. You can review their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local Data: You can delete your supplement logs at any time by clearing the app's cache or uninstalling the app from your device.
  • Server Data: To request the deletion of the anonymous purchase record linked to your device ID, please use our Data Deletion Request Form.

6. Children's Privacy

DosePulse is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
